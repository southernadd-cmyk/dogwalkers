About the idea

This example website presents a fictional local dog walking and daycare company. It is designed to show how a business could use branding, messaging and social media links to promote its services online.

The colour palette gives the page a calm, playful and pet-friendly feel, while the headings and typography aim to make the site feel welcoming and memorable.

Important project note

This website does not advertise a real company.

It has been created purely as a BTEC I.T. project on Using Social Media in Business. The aim is to demonstrate how a company could present itself online, use a clear marketing message, and connect visitors to social media platforms.

The business name, service description and links on this page are all part of a classroom-style example.
